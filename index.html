2

4

✅ Tap your answers

Pick the choice you like, edit it, or write your own. OptionOwl copies your answers so you can keep refining.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

OptionOwl is private by design. We built it so your thinking stays yours. OptionOwl has no user accounts, no sign-up, and no servers of its own, and it does not collect, sell, or share your personal information. This policy explains what that means in plain language.

Information we collect

None. OptionOwl does not collect personal data. There is no account to create and no profile to fill in. Everything you create in the app, including your ideas, prompts, answer sheets, saved AI chat shortcuts, and settings, is stored only on your device. We never receive or see it.

Information we do not collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • No location data.
  • No analytics, usage tracking, or advertising identifiers.
  • No access to your photos, contacts, microphone, or files.

OptionOwl does not track you across apps or websites, and it includes no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Subscriptions and payments

OptionOwl offers an optional auto-renewable subscription, OptionOwl Pro. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. OptionOwl never sees or stores your payment information, billing details, or Apple Account credentials. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Third-party AI services

OptionOwl helps you work with AI chat assistants such as ChatGPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), and Claude (Anthropic). The Chats tab opens these services, or any website you add yourself, in an in-app browser. When you use them, you interact directly with those companies, and anything you choose to send is governed by their own privacy policies and terms. You sign in on their websites; OptionOwl never sees those passwords, and it does not read, store, or transmit your conversations with them.

Data storage and deletion

Your content lives on your device. To delete everything OptionOwl has stored, simply delete the app from your iPhone. There is nothing stored on our side to request or erase, because we never had a copy.

Children's privacy

OptionOwl is rated for users 17 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Security

Because your data never leaves your device and we operate no servers, there is no central database of your information that could be exposed. Your content is protected by your device's own security.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

❓ Frequently asked questions

What is OptionOwl and how do I use it? ›

OptionOwl helps you turn a rough idea or decision into something clear, using an AI chat. (1) Write your idea in the Brainstorm tab. (2) Tap Copy and send it to ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, or any AI, inside OptionOwl or in your own app. (3) Copy the AI's reply back into the Paste tab and tap Build Sheet. (4) Tap your answers in the Answer tab. Send them back to the AI and repeat until your idea is clear.

Do I need an account? ›

No. OptionOwl has no sign-up and no login of its own. Just open the app, finish the short welcome tour, and start. You will sign in to your AI of choice on its own website if you use the built-in Chats tab.

Which AI assistants does it work with? ›

OptionOwl works with any AI chat, including ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude. The Chats tab has built-in shortcuts to those three, and you can add any other AI chat by tapping "Add an AI chat" and entering its website address. You can also just use your favorite AI app outside OptionOwl.

How do the free trial and subscription work? ›

OptionOwl Pro unlocks the full app. The monthly plan starts with a 7-day free trial, then bills monthly. A yearly plan is also available as the best value. Subscriptions renew automatically unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the period ends. You can cancel anytime, and you will keep access until the end of the period you already paid for.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription? ›

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple Account, not inside OptionOwl. On your iPhone: open the Settings app, tap your name at the top, tap Subscriptions, choose OptionOwl, and then change or cancel your plan. You can also tap Manage Subscription on OptionOwl's own Settings tab, which opens the same screen.

I subscribed, but the app still shows the paywall. ›

Tap Restore on the paywall, or go to OptionOwl's Settings tab and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple Account you used to subscribe, and that you have an internet connection. If it still does not unlock, email us and we'll sort it out.

How do I restore a purchase on a new iPhone? ›

Sign in to the new device with the same Apple Account, open OptionOwl, and tap Restore on the paywall or Restore Purchases in Settings. Your OptionOwl Pro access will reactivate.

Is my data private? Where is it stored? ›

Yes. Everything you type stays on your iPhone. OptionOwl has no servers and collects no personal data or analytics. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

The text is too small (or too big). Can I change it? ›

Yes. Open the Settings tab in OptionOwl and use Text size in OptionOwl to make every word bigger or smaller. Tap Reset to return to 100%. OptionOwl also respects your system Dynamic Type setting.

How do I see the welcome tour again? ›

Open the Settings tab and tap Replay Welcome Tour. Your saved ideas, prompts, and answers stay untouched.
